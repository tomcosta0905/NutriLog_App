Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 20, 2026

Macro is an SMS-based nutrition tracking service. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We've written it to be read by humans, not lawyers.

What we collect

When you use Macro, we collect:

  • Your phone number — used to identify your account and send you responses.
  • Your messages — the text you send us describing meals, questions, and preferences.
  • Meal and nutrition data — the food logs and nutritional information we derive from your messages and store on your behalf.
  • Usage data — basic information like message timestamps and whether you're on a free or paid plan.

We do not collect your name, email address, payment details (handled by our payment processor), or any health information beyond what you voluntarily share in your messages.

How we use it

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Macro service — logging meals, tracking nutrition, sending summaries
  • Improve accuracy of food recognition and responses
  • Send you service-related messages (summaries, reminders you've requested, account notices)
  • Manage your subscription and prevent abuse

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your personal information.

Anonymized data

In the future, we may use anonymized, aggregated data — meaning data that cannot be linked back to you — for research or commercial purposes such as food trend analysis. If we do this, we will update this policy and notify users in advance. We will provide an opt-in mechanism so this is always your choice, not ours.

Who we share it with

We share your information only with the third-party services necessary to operate Macro:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery and phone number management
  • Anthropic — AI processing of your meal descriptions
  • Our hosting provider — infrastructure to run the service
  • Our payment processor — to handle subscription billing (they never share your payment details with us)

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or anyone else.

How long we keep it

We retain your meal logs and account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated records may be retained indefinitely.

Your choices

You can:

  • Stop messages at any time by replying STOP to any Macro SMS
  • Request your data by contacting us — we'll send you everything we have
  • Delete your account and data by contacting us
  • Cancel your subscription by replying STOP or contacting us

Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we take this seriously.

Children

Macro is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify you via SMS before the changes take effect. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the current version.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us at privacy@macro.app
